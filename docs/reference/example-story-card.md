# Example: Story card

A worked example of a story card in Linear, written against the [issue template](/reference/issue-template). This story is one of the eight sub-issues under [Example: Epic card](/reference/example-epic-card) — fully filled in so the shape of each section is visible. See [Epics and stories](/reference/epics-and-user-stories) for how stories sit beneath epics.

The example uses the same fictional fruit-catalogue project as the epic mock-up so the two pages read as one continuous worked example.

---

![Story card example](/story-card-example.svg)

*Mock-up of a story card. The body works through all seven sections of the [issue template](/reference/issue-template): why the work matters, where things stand today, what will and won't be done, what counts as done, how to verify it, and the assumption still to confirm.*

Compared with the epic example, the story is tactical: it names the model fields, the rendering behaviour, and the verification steps. The epic carries no implementation detail — that's the deliberate split. **The strategic frame lives on the epic; AI-implementable detail lives on each sub-issue.**
